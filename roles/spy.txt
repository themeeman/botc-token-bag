You are the Spy! <:spy:586693972513259561> You are a Minion on the evil team.

Each night, you will see the whole Grimoire, which contains everyone's characters.

Also, you might register as good and as a Townsfolk or Outsider, even if you are dead. This means that you might show up as good aligned or as any Townsfolk or Outsider to any ability that checks alignment or character.

On the first night, you will be woken up with the other other minions and be informed who the Demon is.
